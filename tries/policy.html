Chart of the Week

Once every two weeks, we will provide commentary on a single chart that represents a characteristic of Japan. Alternatively, a discussion that interprets an international issue from a Japanese perspective will be posted along with the chart. The chart will not be limited to the environment, but will cover topics included in the SDG goals, such as income inequality, urban issues, and gender inequality.
